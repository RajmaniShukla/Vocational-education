Vocational Career

Helping Youth

In our website we will cover all the sectors of vocational training available in India. So, if a student who is confused about choosing his/her career can easily find the best career which suits them. As many of the students in India don’t have the knowledge about vocational training so firstly we will explain what the vocational training is and the benefits of doing it. Then after this we will add all the branches/sections/fields of vocational sectors having all other categories available in it. So, if the student is sure about category in which he/she has to do his vocational training then they can simply go to the categories available in that particular sector and can see all the information such as: 1) Location where such trainings are available 2) Cost-Free or Paid 3) Academic Requirements 4) Job Prospects 5) Job Roles 6) Estimated Wages 7) Career Progression related to these courses. For e.g. If someone is interested in Home Science then he/she can see all the categories available in Home Science such as 1) Dress Making 2) Embroidery & needle work 3) Hair & skin care etc. & if they opt for Dress Making then it covers all the fields as described above. This will help students ut what he/she have to do & he doesn’t have any idea about anything. So, he can take a Psychometric test which will help to measure students suitability for a role based on the required personality characteristics and behavioral style. For Psychometric test Student has to fill all his details which are being asked after this he will get his login I.D & Password using which he can take his Psychometric test. Procedure for Psychometric test: After logging in student has to tell his area of interest, then on the basis of his area of interest Psychometric test will cover 3 rounds:- Round 1: Aptitude & Reasoning Test Aptitude test is used in assessment of student’s logical reasoning and thinking performance & reasoning test measures how well a student can identify a pattern within a large amount of data. Round 2: Verbal Test These tests involve grammar, Verbal analogies. It tests the communication level of a student. Round 3: Psycho Analysis Psycho analysis Test includes personality & mental health test of a student. After the completion of Psychometric test, result will be declared & on the basis of result student will be notified which field and course suits him & all the details of the course that we have described before. Student can also compare two or more courses & choose accordingly. .
